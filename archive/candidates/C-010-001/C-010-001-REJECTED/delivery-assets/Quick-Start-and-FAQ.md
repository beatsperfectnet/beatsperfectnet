# Quick Start and FAQ

## When is a quote ready to send?
Only when `Quote Summary` shows `send_ready`.

## Why did I get `clarify_scope`?
One of the required readiness fields is incomplete or no valid scope row exists.

## Why did I get `below_floor`?
Your quote total is below the minimum profitable floor in `Assumptions`.

## Why did I get `unrealistic_timeline`?
The requested timing is faster than the rush turnaround rule.

## Can I change the deposit percent?
Yes. Update `Assumptions` and the summary recalculates.
