Open Inventory-Tracker-Studio.xlsx, then start on Start Here. Type only in yellow cells. Use the seed examples to understand the flow, then enter your own SKUs and transactions in the blank working rows. Leave formulas, charts, derived reorder points, and reorder outputs alone. Inventory shows fix-stock-data warnings, reorder action, reorder-by date, projected stockout date, days of cover, suggested order quantity, velocity, lead time, safety stock, and derived reorder point. This workbook ships with a few seed examples plus formula-ready capacity for 100 inventory rows, 300 purchase rows, 500 sales rows, 100 calculator rows, and 100 price-list rows.
