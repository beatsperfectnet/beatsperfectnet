Quick Start

1. Open Setup and confirm review date, lookback days, lead time, safety stock days, pack size, fees, and target margin.
2. Review the seed rows, then fill blank Inventory rows with SKU, item, category, supplier, opening stock, unit cost, and notes.
3. Add purchase and sales rows as they happen.
4. If Inventory says Fix stock data, correct stock, purchase, or sales entries before using the reorder recommendation.
5. Read Inventory reorder action, reorder-by date, projected stockout date, days cover, and suggested order quantity.
6. Use Price Calculator before listing a product and save successful prices in Price List.

FAQ
- This workbook does not connect to banks or live inventory services.
- Imported protection behavior can vary by spreadsheet editor.
- Keep one clean copy before making heavy edits.
