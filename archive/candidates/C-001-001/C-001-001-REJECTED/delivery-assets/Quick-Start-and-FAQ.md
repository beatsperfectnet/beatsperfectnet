# Quick Start and FAQ

## Setup
Use cyan/editable cells only. The workbook is designed so normal use does not require formula edits.

## Compatibility
Google Sheets is the primary supported platform. Excel support is only published after final QA confirms formulas and charts survive export.

## Recovery
Keep an untouched original. If formulas or sample data are damaged, make a fresh copy from the original workbook.

## Support scope
Supported: access/download issues, basic setup questions, and clarification of documented use.
Not supported: custom formulas, spreadsheet redesign, personal nutrition advice, meal planning services, or data-entry work.

## Limits
Calories and costs are estimates. The workbook does not connect to live grocery prices and does not provide medical, nutrition, tax, or financial advice.
